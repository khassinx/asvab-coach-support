Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications, software products, and services developed and published by KhassinX (also referred to as "Abraham Alonso," "we," "us," or "our"), including but not limited to Khazen — Personal Finance, ASVAB X, and all future applications developed under the KhassinX brand, whether distributed through the Apple App Store, the Google Play Store, or any other platform.

By downloading, installing, or using any KhassinX application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

Each application may have supplemental disclosures specific to its features. Those disclosures are additive to this policy, not replacements for it.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information strictly necessary to provide the features of each application. The categories of information we may collect include:

Information you provide directly:

  • Account credentials if sign-in is required (e.g., Sign in with Apple — we receive only an anonymized identifier, not your email unless you choose to share it)
  • Financial data you manually enter (transactions, accounts, budgets) — stored locally on your device only
  • Public cryptocurrency wallet addresses you choose to add — stored locally on your device only
  • Preferences, settings, and configuration choices

Information collected automatically:

  • Crash reports and diagnostic data, if you have enabled this through your device's operating system settings
  • Aggregate, anonymized usage statistics through Apple's built-in analytics (if you have opted in on your Apple device) — we cannot identify individual users from this data
  • App version, device type, and operating system version for compatibility and debugging purposes

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Your precise location or GPS coordinates
  • Your contacts, photos, or camera roll (except when you explicitly use a receipt photo feature)
  • Your device's IDFA (Advertising Identifier) or any advertising identifier
  • Browsing history or data from other applications
  • Private keys, seed phrases, or any private cryptographic material
  • Bank credentials — we use Plaid's secure link where applicable, and we never see or store your banking username or password

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the features and functionality of our applications
  • To synchronize your data securely across your own devices via iCloud (only when you enable this)
  • To process and display financial data you enter, entirely on your device
  • To fetch publicly available market data (e.g., cryptocurrency prices from CoinGecko) on your behalf
  • To diagnose crashes and technical issues in order to improve stability
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with applicable law

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any form of behavioral tracking. We do not build user profiles or sell your data to any third party, ever.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties for their own commercial purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may use third-party services that process data on our behalf (e.g., Plaid for bank connectivity, Apple iCloud for cross-device sync). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of KhassinX, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: If KhassinX is acquired, merged, or its assets are transferred, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified via a prominent notice in the application or by email prior to any such transfer.
  • With your explicit consent: We may share information for any other purpose with your prior written consent.

In all other circumstances, your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to KhassinX servers unless a specific feature explicitly requires it (e.g., Plaid sync) and you have opted into that feature.

Data Retention

Because most of your data is stored locally on your device, you control its retention. Deleting the application removes all locally stored data. If iCloud sync is enabled, data in iCloud is governed by Apple's retention policies and can be deleted by you through your iCloud settings.

Any server-side data we may hold (such as Plaid access tokens where applicable) is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at hello@khassinx.com.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at hello@khassinx.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

Some applications may be appropriate for younger users for educational purposes (e.g., exam preparation apps). In such cases, we take additional care to ensure no personal data is collected from minors, and we encourage parents to supervise use.

Third-Party Services

Some KhassinX applications integrate with third-party services to provide functionality. The relevant services and their privacy policies are:

  • Plaid Inc. — Used for secure bank connectivity where applicable. Plaid's Privacy Policy: plaid.com/legal
  • CoinGecko — Used to fetch publicly available cryptocurrency price data. No personal data is transmitted to CoinGecko. CoinGecko's Privacy Policy: coingecko.com/en/privacy
  • Apple Inc. (iCloud, Sign in with Apple, App Store Analytics) — Apple's Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Public Blockchain RPCs — Requests to public blockchain nodes (Mempool.space, Etherscan, Solana RPC) to fetch wallet balances and transaction history are made using your public wallet address only. No personally identifying information is transmitted.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their policies independently.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard safeguards:

  • Sensitive tokens and credentials are stored exclusively in the iOS/macOS Keychain, never in databases or plain storage
  • Local data stores are encrypted using Apple's Data Protection framework (fileProtection(.complete))
  • All network communications use HTTPS/TLS exclusively
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) is supported for application access
  • We do not transmit your financial data to our servers

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your personal information
  • Withdrawal of consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time

Because most data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly by deleting the app or clearing app data through your device settings. For any data we hold server-side, contact us at hello@khassinx.com and we will respond within 30 days.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you
  • The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • The right to limit use of sensitive personal information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not have actual knowledge of selling or sharing personal information of consumers under 16 years of age.

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at hello@khassinx.com with the subject line "California Privacy Rights Request." We will respond within 45 days.

European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent legislation.

Legal basis for processing: We process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the features you request
  • Legitimate interests: Improving application stability and security
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws
  • Consent: Where we explicitly ask for and receive your consent

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. For EU/EEA residents, you may contact your national data protection authority. For UK residents, you may contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

As we are primarily a local-first application developer, most processing occurs on your device. Any cross-border data transfers that do occur (e.g., via Plaid or iCloud) are made to providers that maintain appropriate safeguards under GDPR, including Standard Contractual Clauses.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting a prominent notice within the application
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Where required by law, obtaining your consent before implementing changes

Your continued use of any KhassinX application after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Previous versions of this policy are available upon request by contacting us at hello@khassinx.com.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to resolving complaints and inquiries promptly.
