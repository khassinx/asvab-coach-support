Privacy Policy

ASVAB Coach — by KhassinX

Last updated: March 30, 2026

🔒 ASVAB Coach does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Ever.

Your privacy is fundamental to how ASVAB Coach was designed. This policy explains exactly what data the app uses and why.

What We Collect

Nothing. ASVAB Coach does not collect any personal information, including:

  • Name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to our servers

What Stays on Your Device

All your progress data is stored locally on your device only using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults). This includes:

  • Your practice scores and exam history
  • Study streak and progress statistics
  • Vocabulary mastery status
  • Selected military branch
  • Study plan and diagnostic results

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

AI Chat Feature

ASVAB Coach includes an optional AI Chat feature powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you use this feature:

  • Your messages are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate responses
  • This is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • We do not store your chat history on our servers
  • You can use all other app features without using AI Chat

No Ads. No Tracking. No Accounts.

ASVAB Coach contains no advertising, no third-party tracking SDKs, and requires no account or login. You download it and use it — that's it.

Children's Privacy

ASVAB Coach does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including users under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, any changes would only expand your privacy protections.
